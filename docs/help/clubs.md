# Club Management

## How to create a club
1. Navigate to the "Clubs" section.
2. Click the "Create Club" button.
3. Fill in the required details: Name, Description, Category, City.
4. Click "Create".

## How to create an event
1. Go to your club's detail page.
2. Click on the "Events" tab.
3. Click "Add Event".
4. Fill in the event details and save.

## How to post news
1. Go to your club's detail page.
2. Click on the "News" tab.
3. You can ask the AI Agent to create a post for you, or use the manual "Add Post" button if available.
