# Account Management

## How to reset password
To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". Follow the instructions sent to your email.

## How to change profile picture
1. Go to your profile page.
2. Click on the "Edit Profile" button.
3. Upload a new image in the "Avatar" section.
4. Click "Save".

## How to delete account
Please contact support@unitysphere.com to request account deletion.
